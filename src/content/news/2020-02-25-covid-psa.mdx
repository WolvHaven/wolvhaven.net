**25 February 2020: WUHAN CORONAVIRUS (2019-NCOV)**

A new coronavirus which originated from Wuhan, China called the Novel Coronavirus (2019-NCOV) has spread to various countries & regions around the world, particularly in some of the countries/regions some WolvHaveners reside in.

As this is a topic of public interest, please take into consideration when discussing the subject in any part of WolvHaven.

**🤝 Be Respectful**
Please remember to remain respectful of other members when engaging in discussions regarding the aforementioned topic.

**✅ Fact check**
There have been several cases outside where false information has been disseminated by the public. Always check your sources before sharing information. We do not want to cause unnecessary panic/alarm.

**🏥 Refer to official advisories**
Should you display symptoms, or suspect that someone may be affected, please <ins>refer to the official statements/advisories/announcements released by the relevant departments of your country/state/province.</ins> Do not rely on people here to tell you what to do as such information may vary between different countries/regions.

For more info, you may also visit the World Health Organisation (WHO) website.
https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019
