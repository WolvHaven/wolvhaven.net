## Referendum Exercise – Referendum on Permanent Residency (PR) Implementation

**Voting System:** First Past The Post

**No. of Questions:** 5

**Polling Station(s):** City Hall (4th Haven)

**Polling Dates & Information:** See important dates below

**Question #1:** Should the PR rank be introduced?
- A – Yes
- B – No
<br>

**Question #2:** How should the PR rank be implemented?
- A – Current Citizens shall not be demoted to PR rank.
- B – Citizens who have committed serious crimes or who have a large number of previous offenses shall be demoted to PR rank. 
<br>

**Question #3:** Criteria for promotion to PR rank
- A – Remove the need for an introductory letter and allow PRs with 1 warning to be promoted
- B – No changes to the provisions of the Permanent Residency Act, 2018
<br>

**Question #4:** Criteria for demotion to PR rank
- A – Allow for inactive citizens to be demoted to PR rank
- B – No changes to the provisions of the Permanent Residency Act, 2018
<br>

**Question #5:** Rights and privileges afforded to PRs
- A – Provide PRs with the same rights and privileges as citizens, except the right to participation in parliament and vote at elections
- B – No changes to the provisions of the Permanent Residency Act, 2018
<br>
<br>
